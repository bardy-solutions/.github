## Who we are

Bardy Solutions is a South African software consultancy delivering custom business systems, modernisation projects, integrations, and cloud-ready platforms.

We work with clients who need more than just code — they need a team that can understand the business problem, design the right solution, and deliver reliably.

Our team combines architecture leadership with experienced full-stack delivery across web, mobile, automation, IoT-style operational systems, and VR/simulation projects.
